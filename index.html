About US

Why Our Nasi Kuning Onigiri?

Perfectly balanced with tender ayam betutu and complemented by papaya leaf vegetables, this fusion dish brings the essence of Bali’s culinary heritage to your hands. Ideal for a quick, satisfying meal or snack, our onigiri offers an authentic taste of Bali, wherever you are.
